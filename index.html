About The Book

Transforming Lives Through Faith

"More Than Conquerors" provides churches with a comprehensive blueprint for establishing a substance abuse ministry that combines spiritual truth with practical recovery strategies.

This book addresses the critical need for faith-based addiction recovery programs in our communities, offering step-by-step guidance for implementation.

You'll discover how to create a safe environment for healing, develop effective curriculum, train volunteers, and integrate your ministry with existing church programs.

About The Author

Matthew Lovacheff

With over 15 years of experience in ministry and addiction recovery, Matthew Lovacheff has dedicated his life to helping individuals find freedom through Christ.

As the founder of a successful church-based recovery program, Matthew has witnessed firsthand the transformative power of faith-based addiction ministry.

His passion for equipping churches to address substance abuse comes from both professional experience and personal testimony of God's redeeming grace.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this book suitable for small churches?

Absolutely! The principles in "More Than Conquerors" are scalable and adaptable to churches of all sizes. The book includes specific guidance for small congregations with limited resources.

How is this different from other recovery programs?

While many programs focus either on clinical approaches or spiritual aspects, this book uniquely integrates both within the context of local church ministry, providing a holistic approach to recovery.

Do I need special training to implement this?

Although specialized training is not required, training in group dynamics and discipling are strongly recommended.

Are there workshops and training available for group leaders and ministry staff?

Yes! Matthew and Marlo are available for specialized training. Send an email to matthew@usd21.org to schedule a weekend workshop.
